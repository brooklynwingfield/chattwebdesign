Welcome: Genshin Impact became available for Android, IOS, the Playstation 4, and Windows on September 28, 2020. It was released onto Playstation 5 on April 28, 2021.

Travelers: The travelers are intersteller adventurers. At the beginning of the game, they are separated, so they begin to search the seven nations of Teyvat trying to find the other.

Wishing: There's timed character and weapon banners to wish on, as well as the constant standard banner. Users can pull 3-star, 4-star, or 5-star characters or weapons from said banners.

Travelers

When beginning gameplay, users choose from two twin travelers to play as: Lumine or Aether.

Wishing System

This gacha system lets users use in-game currency to wish on characters and weapons that they can utilize in their gameplay.
