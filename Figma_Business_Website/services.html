Services

Stove Top Academy provides classes for every culinary subject.

Culinary Classes

Cooking schools offer a variety of culinary classes, ranging from basic cooking techniques for beginners to advanced courses for experienced chefs. These classes often cover topics such as knife skills, baking, and pastry techniques.

Cooking Workshops

Cooking workshops are typically shorter, hands-on sessions that focus on specific skills, dishes, or themes. Examples include sushi-making workshops, chocolate tempering, wine and food pairing, and holiday-themed cooking sessions.

Degree Programs

Some cooking schools offer certificate and degree programs in culinary arts, baking and pastry, or hospitality management. These programs provide students with a well-rounded education in the culinary field.

Online Classes

Many cooking schools now offer online cooking classes, allowing students to learn from the comfort of their own homes. These virtual classes cover a wide range of topics and offer flexibility in scheduling.
