Where to Download

Creator

Kyle Seeley is the full creator of this game series. He is the developer and publisher of both games, with even a third game in the series that was completed in 2021.

Emily is Away <3

'Emily is Away <3' is the third game in the game's series that costs $9.99. As the third game, it has even more advanced game mechanics. There are still response choices, multiple endings, and the conclusion of your story with Emily.

Steam

Steam is an application that allows creators to distribute their games for the public to download and play.

Kyle Seeley published 'Emily is Away' onto Steam November 20, 2015. 'Emily is Away Too' was put onto Steam May 26, 2017. And, 'Emily is Away <3' was uploaded April 16, 2021.

Itch.io

Itch.io was likely the beginner platform that Seeley used as it is easier process to upload games onto Itch.io than Steam.

However, the games still remain at the same price as on Steam, and all three games in the series (as well as Seeley's other projects that aren't on Steam) are available for download on their Itch.io page.
