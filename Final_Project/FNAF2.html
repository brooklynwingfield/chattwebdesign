Plot

Similar to the first game, Five Nights at Freddy's 2 shares the same plot. The player needs to use resources in order to survive the night.

Gameplay

The office map is different in this version. There are vents on the left and right side of the office with a oncave in the middle. By using a flashlight in the cave section and the new mask feature to trick the Withered animatronics, along utilizing the same light and door shut options for the vents, players will be able to survive the night.

With the vent angles, players are able to view new locations on the camera.

Just make sure to keep the Puppet's box wound up!

Jumpscares

Jumpscares are still prevalent in the second game, except the format is a bit different. The goal, as before, is to survive until 6AM, or else you will experience a jumpscare.

Lose

Lose Screen- died to an animatronic.

Minigames

A new element that was introduced was minigames after the death screen. Completing the minigames that occur after the death screen allows even more lore to be found.
