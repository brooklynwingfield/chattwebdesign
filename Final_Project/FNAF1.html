Plot

After getting a job as a night guard at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, the player needs to survive five nights in the facility by preventing the restaurant's animatronics from killing you.

Gameplay

The player only stays in one area during the gameplay- the office. This office is equip with a left and right metal door that's activated by button with an outside light feature. Using these tools, alongside perserving power, allows the player to survive from 12AM to 6AM.

By checking the locations in the camera view, players are able to activate the door buttons at approriate times to protect themselves from the animatronics.

Jumpscares

The main visual horror element of the game are the jumpscares. If an animatronic reaches you before 6AM, you will experience a jumpscare and the death screen.
