Plot

The plot of the third game mimics the previous two, except the location changes again. Survive until 6AM by using the in-game resources.

Gameplay

The office layout in the third game changes once again. This time there's an open door on the left side, a huge window in the middle, and a vent on the right side.

There's two views in camera mode: standard view and air ducts. Sealing off the vents prevents Springtrap from getting too close, along with avoiding the Phantom animatronics on the cameras. Players are able to play an audio cue to lure Springtrap away, since there's no door or vent to close in this game.

Jumpscares

Although jumpscares are still present in the third game in the series, Phantom animatronics will not kill you when they jumpscare you, only Springtrap's jumpscare will kill you.

Lose

Lose Screen- died to Springtrap.

Minigames

Just as the second game, the third continues to use minigames after the death screen. Completing the minigames that occur after the death screen allows even more lore to be found.

Endings

Depending on the commitment of player, there's multiple endings that undercover more lore.
